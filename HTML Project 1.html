1.3.1 Void/Non-void tags:
Void tags have no contents
Non void tags have contents and there must be opening tags and closing tags

Examples of void tags:
br tags: for ending a line
img tags: adding pictures; structures:"img src="urls" alt="text""

Examples of non-void tags:
b tag: for making words bold
em tags: for making words italic
a href tags:for making links
